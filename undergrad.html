UNDERGRADUATE COURSES

Overview

Our undergraduate programmes integrate computer science principles with information technology applications,providing students with comprehensive understanding in both fields.It equips them with knowledge and skills necessary for a successful career in the dynamic and evolving technology industry.

Courses

Common courses offered for both BSc Information Technology and BSc Computer Science include:

Level 100

  • DCIT101:
    Introduction To Computer Science
  • DCIT102:
    Computer Hardware and Circuit
  • DCIT103:
    Introduction To Computer Science
  • DCIT104:
    Programming Fundamentals

Level 200

  • DCIT201:
    Programming I
  • DCIT205:
    Multimedia and Web Design
  • DCIT204:
    Data Structures & Algorithm I
  • DCIT202:
    Introduction To Mobile Application Development

Level 300

  • DCIT301:
    Operating Systems
  • DCIT305:
    Database Fundamentals
  • DCIT304:
    Research Methods
  • DCIT302:
    Human Computer Interaction

Level 400

  • DCIT400:
    Project
  • DCIT409:
    Digital Forensics
  • DCIT401:
    Social,Legal,Ethical and Professional Issues
  • DCIT402:
    Management Principles in Computing

Practical Experience

Hands-on projects,internsips,and final year projects provide students with real-world experience,enhancing their problem solving and teamwork skills.
